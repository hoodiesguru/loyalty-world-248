The Effect of Page Experience on Modern UX

Published by Ashley Brooks | July 17, 2026 | ⏰ 1 min read

Google's shift towards user experience metrics means that page speed, rendering smoothness, and interactivity are paramount. These metrics track LCP, FID, and CLS.

Static sites natively excel at these metrics by serving pre-built HTML. This structural advantage allows digital marketing assets to naturally beat slower, dynamic CMS frameworks.
